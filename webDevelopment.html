        Deliverables: Needs assessment report, Project scope document, Project roadmap

        Design services

        We create wireframes and prototypes to ensure a user-friendly UI/UX experience. Our team designs the visual aspects of the web application.

        Deliverables: Wireframes, Prototypes, Style guides, User research and analysis

        Development services

        We provide frontend, backend, and full-stack development services, implementing the visual and interactive aspects of the web app.

        Deliverables: Requirement analysis, Front-end development, Back-end development
