        Ask Me Anything

        Answers to questions I frequently receive

        What inspired you to start this company?

        I saw a gap in the market for technology that truly prioritized human needs over profit margins. I wanted to create a company that would build solutions with real social impact, not just commercial success.

        How do you balance innovation with ethics?

        Ethics isn't a constraint on innovation—it's a foundation for it. We have an ethics board that reviews all major projects, and we've built ethical considerations into our development process from day one.

        What's the biggest challenge you've faced as a founder?

        Maintaining our values during rapid growth has been challenging. As we've scaled, I've worked hard to ensure that our commitment to ethical innovation and inclusive culture remains at the core of everything we do.

        How do you stay ahead of technological trends?

        I dedicate time each week to reading research papers, attending conferences, and having conversations with experts outside our industry. I also believe in empowering our team to explore emerging technologies and bring their insights to the table.

        What advice would you give aspiring entrepreneurs?

        Start with a problem you genuinely care about solving. Building a company is hard work, and passion for your mission will carry you through the challenges. Also, surround yourself with people who complement your skills and share your values.
